Welcome to Siren’s Muse

I'm Aurelia Hawthorne, the artist at the heart of Siren's Muse Tattoo. I bring years of experience and a profound love for the craft to create stunning, personalized tattoos. Inspired by the mystical allure of mermaids and sirens, my work blends modern design with mythical elements.

As a sole artist–run studio, I’m dedicated to crafting tattoos that meet and exceed your expectations. I ensure a safe, comfortable, and memorable experience from consultation to completion.
