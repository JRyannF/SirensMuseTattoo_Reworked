Meet the Artist: Aurelia Hawthorne

As the sole proprietor of my studio, I pour my heart and soul into crafting tattoos that not only meet but exceed your wildest expectations. With an unwavering dedication to innovation and creativity, I push the boundaries of tattooing—specializing in American traditional, full-color designs, black and gray, and black-and-gray realism. Each piece is a labor of love, meticulously designed and executed with the utmost attention to detail.

With a double major in Fine Art and Biology (focused on Anatomy and Physiology) and 12 years of tattooing experience, I bring a unique blend of artistic vision and scientific precision to every project.

From our initial consultation to the final session, I’m committed to guiding you through every step. Your safety and comfort are paramount, which is why Siren’s Muse Tattoo maintains a clean, sanitary environment where you can feel confident and at ease. Embark on your tattoo journey with me, and let’s bring your artistic visions to life in vibrant color and intricate detail.
